BANDARBAN

Overview

5 Days 6 Nights

Welcome to Bandarban, a captivating destination nestled within the verdant landscapes of Bangladesh's Chittagong Hill Tracts. Known for its mystic charm, Bandarban enchants travelers with its rolling hills, dense forests, and serene rivers. Immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of indigenous cultures, where vibrant traditions and warm hospitality beckon. From the iconic Nilgiri Hills offering sweeping vistas to picturesque villages like Ruma and Rangamati, Bandarban invites you to explore its natural wonders and engage in thrilling treks. This tranquil haven captures the essence of Bangladesh's hill country, inviting you to embrace its beauty, culture, and tranquility.

Day 1: Arrival and Introduction to Bandarban

  • Arrive in Bandarban, the heart of the Chittagong Hill Tracts.
  • Check in to your accommodation and unwind from your journey.
  • Visit the iconic Nilgiri Hills for a stunning sunset view.
  • Engage in a local cultural exchange with indigenous communities.

Day 2: Discovering Hillside Treasures

  • Start the day with a hearty breakfast
  • Embark on a trek to Boga Lake, a hidden jewel surrounded by lush hills.
  • Enjoy a picnic lunch by the pristine lake and take in the serene surroundings.
  • Return to your accommodation and relax.

Day 3: Than Chi Exploration

  • Travel to Than Chi, a captivating hillside village known for its unique culture and hospitality.
  • Immerse yourself in the local way of life and engage with the indigenous community.
  • Explore the vibrant markets and discover traditional crafts.

Day 4: Indigenous Villages and Waterfalls

  • Venture to remote indigenous villages like Ruma or Bawm.
  • Immerse yourself in their unique cultures and way of life.
  • Visit Chimbuk Hill, the highest peak in Bangladesh, for breathtaking panoramic views.
  • Discover the beauty of Nafakhum Waterfall, cascading amidst dense forests.

Day 5: Departure with Lasting Memories

  • Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and soak in the tranquil ambiance.
  • Visit the Buddhist Golden Temple (Buddha Dhatu Jadi) and offer your respects.
  • Stroll through Bandarban town, exploring local markets and trying traditional cuisine.
  • Depart with cherished memories and a deeper connection to the enchanting landscapes and cultures of Bandarban..
