SAINT MARTIN

Overview

2 Days 3 Nights

Discover Saint Martin Island, a tranquil tropical gem nestled in the Bay of Bengal. With its pristine beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and mesmerizing sunsets, this idyllic escape promises a serene retreat for beach lovers and nature enthusiasts. Explore crystal-clear waters, indulge in fresh seafood, and embrace the warmth of local hospitality on this captivating island paradise.

Day 1: Island Arrival and Coastal Delights

  • Arrive at Saint Martin Island, welcomed by its pristine beaches and turquoise waters.
  • Spend your morning lounging on the dazzling shores, basking in the sun's warmth.
  • After a seaside lunch, embark on a snorkeling adventure to explore the vibrant coral reefs.
  • As the day winds down, take a leisurely walk along the shoreline and witness a breathtaking sunset.
  • Enjoy a delectable seafood dinner under the stars, savoring the island's culinary delights.

Day 2: Exploring and Departure

  • Catch the sunrise over the bay, starting your day with a magical view.
  • After breakfast, set out for a thrilling boat excursion to Chera Dwip, a nearby uninhabited island.
  • Engage in beachcombing, swimming, and relaxation on the pristine sands of Chera Dwip.
  • Return to Saint Martin Island for a flavorful lunch.
  • Spend your afternoon unwinding on the beach, collecting seashells, or simply reading a book.
  • Bid farewell to the island's tranquility as you depart, taking with you cherished memories of your brief but captivating Saint Martin getaway.
