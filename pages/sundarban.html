SUNDARBAN

Overview

3 Days 4 Nights

Discover the mystical Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the largest mangrove forest on Earth. Home to elusive Bengal tigers and diverse wildlife, this unique ecosystem weaves through tidal waterways and lush forests. Embark on riveting boat safaris, encountering saltwater crocodiles, deer, and a variety of birds. The Sundarbans offers a captivating journey into nature's intricate dance between land and water, where every moment is an exploration of the wild and untamed.

Day 1: Mangrove Enchantment

  • Arrive at the gateway to the Sundarbans and meet your guide.
  • Board a boat and venture into the heart of the mangrove forest.
  • Navigate through winding waterways, surrounded by towering mangrove trees.
  • Look out for wildlife, such as spotted deer and playful animals
  • Reach your eco-friendly accommodation, surrounded by the serene beauty of the Sundarbans.

Day 2: Royal Bengal Tigers and River Expeditions

  • Begin an early morning boat safari in search of the elusive royal Bengal tigers.
  • Marvel at the natural beauty of the forest and its inhabitants.
  • Cruise along picturesque rivers, observing saltwater crocodiles and diverse bird species.
  • Enjoy a picnic lunch amidst nature's splendor.
  • Return to your accommodation, where the tranquil ambience sets the stage for an evening of relaxation.

Day 3: Village Exploration and Farewell

  • Visit a local village to gain insights into the unique way of life of the Sundarbans' inhabitants.
  • Engage in a cultural exchange, learning about traditional practices and daily routines.
  • Explore the vibrant marketplaces and witness the resilience of the local community.
