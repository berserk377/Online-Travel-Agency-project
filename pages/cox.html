Cox Bazar

Overview

4 Days 5 Nights

Cox's Bazar, located on the southeastern coast of Bangladesh, is a picturesque and renowned destination that captivates travelers with its stunning natural beauty, expansive beaches, and rich cultural heritage. As the world's longest unbroken sandy beach, Cox's Bazar offers an idyllic escape for both relaxation and exploration.

Day 1: Arrival and Beach Bliss

  • Arrive at Cox's Bazar and check into your chosen accommodation.
  • Spend the morning relaxing on the iconic Cox's Bazar Beach, sunbathing and enjoying the soothing waves.
  • Afternoon visit to Himchari National Park: Explore the park's walking trails, visit Himchari Waterfall, and marvel at the diverse flora and fauna.

Day 2: Beach Activities and Cultural Exploration

  • Engage in water sports activities like parasailing, jet skiing, and banana boat rides.
  • Visit the Marine Aquarium and Fisheries Biology Center to learn about marine life and conservation efforts.
  • Afternoon exploration of Cox's Bazar town: Wander through local markets, try traditional Bangladeshi street food, and visit nearby Buddhist temples.
  • Evening cultural performance: Enjoy a traditional dance or music performance showcasing local culture.

Day 3: Inani Beach and Coastal Delights

  • Early morning departure to Inani Beach: Experience the pristine shores and indulge in beach photography.
  • Lunch with a view: Enjoy a beachside picnic with fresh seafood and local delicacies.
  • Optional boat ride: Explore nearby islands and coastal landmarks from the water.
