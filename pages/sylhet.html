Sylhet

Overview

3 Days 4 Nights

Discover Sylhet, an enchanting corner of northeast Bangladesh. A tapestry of lush tea gardens, serene landscapes, and vibrant culture awaits. Explore the tea capital of Srimangal, immerse in Sylhet city's blend of tradition and modernity, and uncover natural gems like Ratargul Swamp Forest and Lalakhal. Sylhet invites you to experience the essence of Bangladesh's beauty and heritage.

Day 1: Arrival and Tea Garden Serenity

  • Arrive in Sylhet and check in to your chosen accommodation.
  • Start your journey with a visit to Srimangal, the "Tea Capital."
  • Wander through lush tea gardens, learning about the tea production process.
  • Visit the Lawachara National Park, home to diverse wildlife and beautiful trails.
  • Enjoy a traditional Sylheti dinner and immerse yourself in the local flavors.

Day 2: Sylhet City and Spiritual Wonders

  • Depart for Sylhet city, a hub of cultural heritage and modernity.
  • Explore Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, known for its stunning architecture.
  • Visit Hazrat Shah Jalal Mazar and Hazrat Shah Paran Mazar, two revered Sufi shrines.
  • Stroll through the bustling Zindabazar market, a vibrant center of trade.

Day 3: Natural Gems and Departure

  • Begin your day with an early departure to Ratargul Swamp Forest.
  • Enjoy a serene boat ride through the mangrove forest, surrounded by tranquility.
  • Visit Lalakhal, renowned for its emerald waters and breathtaking landscapes.
  • Indulge in a farewell lunch by the river.
  • Depart from Sylhet, taking with you memories of its captivating landscapes and rich culture
